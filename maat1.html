Module 1 • Law Before Force • Ori Discipline

Law Before Force

The Warrior installs Ma’at as operating law before learning power. This module trains you to correct yourself within 24 hours and prevents you from becoming the Ajogun you oppose.

Focus: Truth + Integrity Rule: Correction in 24h Mode: Daily accountability
SGI Doctrine: A Warrior without law becomes Ajogun.

Core Confessions

1 • 8 • 16 • 24 • 34 • 42

Required Memorization

Confessions: 1, 8, 42

Daily Time

8–12 minutes (AM read + PM log)

Lesson Objectives

  • Install Ma’at as non-negotiable law (not mood, not preference).
  • Use Ori as the internal judge (self-audit before blame).
  • Apply the SGI correction rule: Fix it within 24 hours.

This module is not about perfection. It’s about accountability speed.

Practices

  • Morning Recitation: read today’s confession aloud (1 minute).
  • Speech Restraint Drill: no exaggeration, no “extra words,” no hidden insult.
  • Pause Protocol: before action, ask: “Is this Ma’at or ego?”

If you violate: correct immediately (apology, repair, restitution, silence).

Assignment

Write 7–12 lines answering:

  • Where did Ma’at restrain impulse today?
  • What would I have done without law?
  • What correction did I perform (if needed)?

Module 1 Confessions

  • 1 — I have not committed sin.
  • 8 — I have not uttered lies.
  • 16 — I am not a man of deceit.
  • 24 — I have not transgressed the law.
  • 34 — I have not been arrogant.
  • 42 — I have not spoken arrogantly.
