Warriors Mini Course

A pre-initiation course into the Warriors of Isese/ Orisha/ Ifa that focuses on the distinction between Ajagun (training pressure) and Ajogun (affliction through disorder), and the function of the Warriors as conscious forces of alignment that protect Ori and destiny. Satisfactory completion of this mini-course prepares an aborisha for initiation into the Warriors and is required for consideration as a candidate for 'Warrior Initiation Certification-Readiness'. Not everyone needs all of the Warriors. Some initiates need 1-warrior and not the others. Other initiates need the other warriors and not the one. All Initiations MUST by confirmed by divination prior to commencing.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand Ajagun (energies of challenge)
  • Understand Ajogun (energies of affliction)
  • Understand Warriors (Forces of Nature) and Warrior function
  • Apply sacrifice, vows, and accountability to sustain Warrior forces
