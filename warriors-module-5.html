Module 5 — Sacrifice, Vows & Accountability

Warriors are sustained by what you give up, what you swear to, and what you answer for. This module closes the course by establishing ongoing structures that keep Warrior forces active.

Personal Sacrifice

  • Removes excess and distraction
  • Builds discipline, focus, discernment, awareness
  • Strengthens the Warriors by training restraint

Sacred Vows

  • Create binding commitments
  • Authorize Warrior intervention
  • Establish boundaries and define conduct

Accountability

  • Sustains discipline over time
  • Prevents relapse and stabilizes alignment
  • Stops Ajagun from becoming Ajogun
Functional Truth: Sacrifice trains the Warriors • Vows authorize the Warriors • Accountability keeps the Warriors active.
