Module 4 — The Four Warriors

These are functional archetypes of protection. Learn what each Warrior does in real life—not as mythology, but as operating principles.

Ogun

DisciplineIronWill
  • Guardian of tools, labor, technology
  • Cuts through illusion and hesitation
  • Teaches restraint before power

Practice: One disciplined action with tools/time today (no excuses).

Ochossi

JusticePrecisionLiberation
  • Spirit of the hunt and moral focus
  • Frees those trapped or unjustly bound
  • Never wastes energy or motion

Practice: Remove one inefficiency and aim at one clean target.

Eshu

IntelligenceDiscernmentChoice
  • Governs crossroads and consequence
  • Tests awareness before conflict
  • Teacher of warriors, not a fighter

Practice: Before any decision, name the consequence you accept.

Osun

Protection of OriWarningStillness
  • Embodies intuition and foresight
  • Signals danger before it manifests
  • Requires quiet awareness and restraint

Practice: 10 minutes of stillness; write the first warning you sense.

Assignment: Which Warrior is most active in your life right now—and which one is weakest? Explain with real evidence.
