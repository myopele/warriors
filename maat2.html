Module 2 • Ajagun Discipline • Force + Restraint

Force, Power & Restraint

A Warrior learns to hold force without intimidation, without coercion, and without cruelty. This module trains controlled strength so you don’t become violence while opposing violence.

Focus: Controlled Strength Prohibition: Intimidation Mode: Non-reaction training
SGI Doctrine: Power used without restraint is corruption.

Core Confessions

2 • 4 • 15 • 23 • 28

7-Day Rule

No threats • no coercion • no fear tactics

Daily Time

10–15 minutes (stillness + log)

Lesson Objectives

  • Differentiate authority from fear.
  • Train “hold power” capacity without discharge.
  • Replace escalation with quiet enforcement.

Practices

  • Standing Stillness: 3–5 minutes daily (no fidget, no phone).
  • Anger Observation: notice heat rise; do not act until it cools.
  • No Intimidation: 7-day discipline (tone, posture, words).

Assignment

Log one moment where force was withheld:

  • What triggered you?
  • What would intimidation have done?
  • What did restraint protect?

Module 2 Confessions

  • 2 — I have not committed robbery with violence.
  • 4 — I have not slain men and women.
  • 15 — I have not attacked any person.
  • 23 — I have not caused terror.
  • 28 — I have not acted with violence.
