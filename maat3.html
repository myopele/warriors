Module 3 • Inner Warfare • Speech + Emotion + Mind

Inner Warfare

Ajogun exploit breaches in speech, emotion, and mental discipline. This module seals the leaks: no slander, no unnecessary words, no emotional discharge that becomes spiritual vulnerability.

SGI Doctrine: Most battles are lost internally first.

Core Confessions

10 • 13 • 14 • 19 • 20 • 25 • 32

Key Drill

Minimal speech day + silence after provocation

Daily Time

10 minutes (trigger + recovery log)

Practices

  • Minimal Speech Day: speak only what is necessary (24 hours).
  • Silence After Provocation: 90 seconds of no reply, then choose law.
  • Trigger Map: name your top 3 triggers and your recovery method.

Assignment

Identify your primary internal Ajogun (choose one):

  • Anger / Irritation
  • Pride / Arrogance
  • Envy / Comparison
  • Fear / Avoidance
  • Deceit / Performance

Write 8–12 lines: “How it enters, what it costs, how I close the gate.”

Module 3 Confessions

  • 10 — I have not uttered curses.
  • 13 — I have not caused anyone to weep.
  • 14 — I have not eaten my heart (greed / deceit).
  • 19 — I have not slandered anyone.
  • 20 — I have not been angry without just cause.
  • 25 — I have not been wrathful.
  • 32 — I have not multiplied words excessively.

Pass Condition

  • Reduced emotional reaction time.
  • Improved recovery speed.
  • Less “extra speech” under stress.
