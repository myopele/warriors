Module 4 • Stewardship • Energy + Resources + Rank

Stewardship & Leadership

Warriors conserve energy, protect resources, and treat rank as responsibility—never entitlement. This module trains clean exchange, clean sexuality, and leadership ethics that do not exploit.

SGI Doctrine: Leakage of energy equals loss of authority.

Core Confessions

3 • 5 • 9 • 11 • 12 • 17 • 36

Key Drill

Energy + resource log (daily)

Daily Time

10 minutes (log + discipline)

Practices

  • Sexual Discipline: no impulsive discharge; choose intentional channeling.
  • Resource Ethics: take only what is assigned; no “small theft.”
  • Rank Audit: if you lead, your tone must protect—not crush.

If you fail: repair immediately (restitution, apology, correction).

Assignment

Create a 7-day log with 3 lines per day:

  • Where did I conserve energy today?
  • Where did I waste/consume without awareness?
  • Where did I lead ethically (or fail and repair)?

Module 4 Confessions

  • 3 — I have not stolen.
  • 5 — I have not stolen grain.
  • 9 — I have not carried away food.
  • 11 — I have not committed adultery.
  • 12 — I have not committed sexual perversion.
  • 17 — I have not stolen cultivated land.
  • 36 — I have not caused harm to servants.

Pass Condition

  • Observable reduction in energy leakage.
  • Clean exchange and honest accounting.
  • Leadership expressed as protection and fairness.
