Warriors Mini Course

A focused pre-initiation into the Warriors focuses on the distinction between Ajagun (training pressure) and Ajogun (affliction through disorder), and the function of the Warriors as conscious forces of alignment that protect Ori and destiny. Satisfactory completion of this mini-course prepares an aborisha for initiation into the Warriors and is required for consideration as a candidate for 'Warrior Initiation Certification-Readiness'. All Initiation MUST by confirmed by divination.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand Ajagun (energies of challenge)
  • Understand Ajogun (energies of affliction)
  • Understand Warriors and Warrior function
  • Apply sacrifice, vows, and accountability to sustain Warrior forces
