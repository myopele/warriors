Module 3 — The Warriors & Ajagun / Ajogun

Ajagun are neutral energies of testing alignment. Ajogun are energies of affliction arising when discipline and awareness have failed. Warriors teach correct response, prevent decay, and restore order.

Ajagun & Warriors

Ajagun apply pressure and conditions that demand growth. Warriors teach how to meet Ajagun correctly.

  • Ajagun apply pressure — Warriors teach response
  • Ajagun test readiness — Warriors build mastery
  • Ajagun initiate struggle — Warriors support devotion
Key Teaching: Ajagun test alignment. Warriors train you for balance. Mastery is born when both are understood.
Training Pressure (Neutral Test)

Ajogun & Warriors

Ajogun are conditions of disorder and imbalance. Warriors prevent, correct, and contain Ajogun (excess).

  • Ajogun drain life-energy — Warriors protect life-force
  • Ajogun create stagnation/decay — Warriors restore progress
  • Ajogun thrive in neglect/chaos — Warriors require discipline/order
  • Ajogun multiply when ignored — Warriors resolve imbalance
Key Teaching: Ajogun warn that order has failed. Warriors restore what was imbalanced.
Affliction Condition (Must Resolve)
