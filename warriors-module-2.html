Module 2 — Ajagun vs Ajogun

Ajagun are energy frequencies that strengthen and train. Ajogun are energy frequencies that weaken and drain. Mastery begins when you correctly label pressure: training vs affliction.

Ajagun — Energies of Challenge

Strengthen Train Mastery Required
  • Initiate struggle and growth
  • Catalysts for destiny activation
  • Train discipline, courage, and will
  • Necessary for initiation and priesthood
  • Strengthen Ori through conscious confrontation and transformation

Ajogun — Energies of Affliction

Weaken Drain Must Resolve
  • Forces of imbalance, obstruction, and disintegration
  • Cause confusion, stagnation, and decay
  • Feed on neglect and unconsciousness
  • Must be corrected, restrained, or removed
  • Weaken Ori and derail destiny — resolved by Know ThySelf (again)
Functional Truth: Ajagun = training pressure. Ajogun = unresolved disorder. Your job is to prevent Ajagun from becoming Ajogun through neglect.
